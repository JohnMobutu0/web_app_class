Samsung Ring

Samsung's Galaxy Ring is a wearable technology designed for health and fitness tracking. It comes in three finishes and comes in eight sizes. The device has multiple sensors for monitoring heart rate, blood oxygen levels, skin temperature, and sleep patterns. It uses AI to provide personalized health insights through the Samsung Health app. The Galaxy Ring offers up to 14 days of battery life and features a virtual health score, cycle tracking, and automatic workout detection. It is compatible with Samsung devices and is expected to ship by the end of July 2024.
