Samsung Galaxy Young

The Samsung Galaxy Young is a budget-friendly Android smartphone released in March 2013. It features a compact design with a 3.27-inch TFT LCD display, a 1.0 GHz Cortex-A5 processor, 768MB RAM, and 4GB internal storage. It has a 3.15 MP rear camera capable of 480p video recording and no front camera. The device runs on Android 4.1.2 Jelly Bean with Samsung's TouchWiz interface and supports GSM and HSPA networks. It also has Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 3.0, GPS, and a microUSB 2.0 port. Some models support dual SIM functionality.
