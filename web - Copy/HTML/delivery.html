TechShop pick and pack your items, even gift-wrapping eligible packages upon request. Once packed, your order joins a line haul trailer alongside many other customer orders. These trailers transport packages to TechShop Air sites, where they’re loaded onto aircraft. TechShop's fleet of over 110 aircraft ensures efficient transportation. When an aircraft lands at its destination, the next step in the journey is a sort center. Here, packages are sorted by ZIP code before being sent to delivery stations or partner facilities like U.S. Post Offices. At the delivery station, an TechShop employee organizes packages based on delivery routes. Delivery drivers then load the packages onto their vehicles. When your package is en route, you receive notifications, and it’s delivered to your front door or another pickup point, such as an TechShop Locker.
