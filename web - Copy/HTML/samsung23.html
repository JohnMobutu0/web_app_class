Samsung

The Samsung Galaxy S23 is a high-end smartphone with a Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor, 8GB of RAM, and a 6.1-inch Full HD+ display with a resolution of 2340 x 1080 pixels. It features a triple camera setup with a 50MP wide-angle camera, 12MP ultra-wide camera, and a 10MP telephoto camera with 3x optical zoom. The front camera is 12MP, ideal for selfies and video calls. The phone has a 3900mAh battery, providing up to 22 hours of video playback. Its design is built with an Armor Aluminum frame and Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2 for durability. The operating system is Android with Samsung's One UI. Prices start around $597.49 for the 128GB model.
