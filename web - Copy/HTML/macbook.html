MacBook

The MacBook Pro 14-inch is a powerful laptop designed for professionals and power users. It features M1 Pro and M1 Max chips, up to 10-core CPUs and 32-core GPUs, and unified memory for seamless multitasking. The 14-inch model features a Liquid Retina XDR display with a resolution of 3024 x 1964 pixels. It offers a long battery life, up to 17 hours of web browsing and 11 hours of video playback. The laptop is compact and lightweight, with ports such as Thunderbolt 4, HDMI, and an SDXC card slot. It also features a six-speaker sound system with force-canceling woofers and a 1080p FaceTime HD camera for clear video calls. Prices vary by configuration, with the 2023 model priced around $1,699.95.
