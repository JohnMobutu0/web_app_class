Iphone 5

The iPhone 5 is a significant model in Apple's iPhone lineup, known for its design and technological advancements. Released on September 21, 2012, it features a slimmer, lighter design with an aluminum body and a 4-inch Retina display. The processor is powered by the Apple A6 chip, providing faster performance and better energy efficiency. The device comes with 1GB of RAM and storage options of 16GB, 32GB, and 64GB. It has a 1440mAh battery, providing up to 8 hours of talk time on 3G and 225 hours of standby time.
