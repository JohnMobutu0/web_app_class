Smart Dog

The TECNO Dynamic 1 is a robotic dog inspired by German Shepherds, designed to perform complex movements like climbing stairs and jumping. It has a torque output of 45 Newton meters per kilogram and is equipped with AI and sensors for navigation and interaction. The Dynamic 1 supports Wi-Fi 6 and Bluetooth 5.2, allowing seamless connectivity with smartphones and other devices. It can be controlled via a smartphone app, allowing users to issue commands and monitor its activities. It has an internal 15,000mAh battery, providing around 90 minutes of usage. It also features voice recognition and a headlight system. The Dynamic 1 showcases TECNO's commitment to robotics and AI innovation.
