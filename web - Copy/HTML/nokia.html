Nokia

The TECNO Dynamic 1 is a robotic dog inspired by German Shepherds, designed to perform complex movements like climbing stairs and The Nokia 3310 is a classic mobile phone known for its durability and simplicity. First released in September 2000, it features a monochrome display with a resolution of 84 x 48 pixels and a long battery life of 900mAh. It includes basic features like SMS messaging, a calculator, a stopwatch, and the iconic Snake game. The phone has sold 126 million units worldwide, making it one of the best-selling phones of all time. A modern version was released in 2017, featuring a 2.4-inch color display, 2MP rear camera, 16MB internal storage, and support for 2G and 3G networks.
